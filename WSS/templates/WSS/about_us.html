About WSS
WSS is a two-day scientific event consisting of scientific talks, workshops, discussion panels, and poster presentations in computer science and engineering. It is aimed at providing an environment in which Iranian academics and researchers from all around the world could exchange their knowledge, present their latest work, share their experience, and expand their network.
